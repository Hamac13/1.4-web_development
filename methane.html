Causes and Effects of Methane

Causes of methane

Methane is produced by the transportation of fossil fuels and the digestion process of ruminants which are animals which digest food with microbes in the digestive tract called rumen

Effects of methane

Methane has a much higher global warming potential than carbon dioxide 21 times worse so it is among th worst greenhouse gases
